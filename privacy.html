Privacy Policy for Neon Snek

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Infinite Outlines, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Neon Snek mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly from you. We do not require you to create an account to play Neon Snek.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

Our Service uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Specifically, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as your device's advertising ID and usage data.

For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

3. Local Storage

The Service may use local storage via Capacitor.js to save your high scores and game preferences locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
